##3. World Politics & History##

**Revolutions:**
- **French Revolution (1789):**
-- Date: 14 July 1789 (Fall of Bastille).
-- Slogan: Liberty, Equality, Fraternity.
-- Child of Revolution: Napoleon Bonaparte.
-- Result: Rise of Capitalism.
- **Russian Revolution (1917):**
-- Also called: Bolshevik Revolution / October Revolution.
-- Leader: Vladimir Lenin.
-- Result: Rise of Socialism / End of Tsarist Rule.
- **Cultural Revolution:** China.
- **Arab Spring (2010)::** Started in Tunisia (Jasmine Revolution).

**World Wars:**
- **WWI (1914-1918):**
-- US President: Woodrow Wilson (14 Points).
-- Ended with: Treaty of Versailles (1919).
-- Fall of Empires: Ottoman, Austro-Hungarian, Russian, German.
- **WWII (1939-1945):**
-- Started: 1 Sept 1939 (Germany invaded Poland).
-- Ended: 2 Sept 1945 (Japan surrendered).
-- US President: FDR (start), Harry Truman (end - ordered nuke).
-- Holocaust: Genocide of Jews by Nazis.

**Important Treaties:**
- **Balfour Declaration (1917):** British support for a Jewish homeland in Palestine.
- **Camp David Accords (1978):** Egypt-Israel Peace Treaty (Anwar Sadat & Menachem Begin).
- **Kyoto Protocol:** Environment (Greenhouse gas reduction).
- **Montreal Protocol:** Environment (Ozone layer protection).
- **Paris Agreement:** Climate Change.
- **Ramsar Convention:** Wetlands conservation (1971).

##6. Civilizations##
- **Mesopotamian:** Oldest civilization (Iraq). Tigris & Euphrates rivers. Sumerians invented wheel & cuneiform writing.
- **Egyptian:** Nile river. Pyramids, Mummies, Hieroglyphics (sacred writing), Papyrus.
- **Indus Valley:** Harappa & Mohenjo-daro. Urban planning, drainage system.
- **Chinese:** Great Wall, Paper, Gunpowder, Compass.
- **Greek:** Democracy (Athens), Olympics (776 BC), Philosophy.
- **Inca:** Peru (Machu Picchu).
- **Maya:** Central America (Calendar).
