##4. Organizations & Headquarters##
**United Nations (UN):**
- **Founded:** 24 Oct 1945.
- **Members:** 193 (Latest: South Sudan).
- **Official Languages (6):** English, French, Chinese, Russian, Spanish, Arabic.
- **Veto Powers (P5):** US, UK, France, Russia, China.

**Regional Organizations:**
- **SAARC:** HQ Kathmandu (Nepal). Founded 1985 in Dhaka.
- **ASEAN:** HQ Jakarta (Indonesia). Members: 10.
- **EU:** HQ Brussels (Belgium). Monetary Union (Euro).
- **NATO:** Military Alliance against USSR. Members: 32 (Latest Sweden). Muslim members: Turkey, Albania.
- **OPEC:** Oil exporting countries. Founded in Baghdad.
- **BIMSTEC:** HQ Dhaka.
- **Greenpeace:** Environmental NGO (HQ Amsterdam).

**Intelligence Agencies:**
- **CIA:** USA.
- **KGB (Old)/FSB:** Russia.
- **Mossad:** Israel.
- **RAW:** India.
- **ISI:** Pakistan.
- **MI6:** UK.
