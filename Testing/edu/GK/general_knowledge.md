##5. Famous Personalities & Quotes##
**Philosophers:**
- **Socrates:** "Know Thyself", "Virtue is Knowledge". (Drank Hemlock).
- **Plato:** Author of *"The Republic"*. Academy in Athens.
- **Aristotle:** Author of *"The Politics"*. Lyceum in Athens. Father of Biology/Political Science. "Man is a political animal."
- **Karl Marx:** "History of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles."

**Leaders:**
- **Abraham Lincoln:** "Democracy is government of the people, by the people, for the people." (Gettysburg Address).
- **Nelson Mandela:** Author of *"Long Walk to Freedom"*. First black president of SA.
- **Julius Caesar:** "Veni, Vidi, Vici" (I came, I saw, I conquered).
- **Napoleon:** "Give me a good mother, I will give you a good nation." "Impossible is a word found only in the dictionary of fools."

##10. Miscellaneous Important Facts##
- **Blue Riband:** Award for fastest Atlantic crossing.
- **Pulitzer Prize:** Journalism.
- **Booker Prize:** Literature.
- **Academy Awards (Oscars):** Film.
- **Nobel Prize:** Started 1901. Economics added 1969.
- **Black Cat:** Commandos of India.
- **Black September:** Palestinian guerrilla group.
- **Seven Sisters:** 7 NE states of India (Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram, Manipur, Nagaland, Arunachal).
- **Golden Triangle:** Opium zone (Laos, Myanmar, Thailand).
- **Golden Crescent:** Opium zone (Pakistan, Afghanistan, Iran).
