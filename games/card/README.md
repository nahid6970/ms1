# Neon Blackjack

A premium, high-stakes Blackjack experience built with modern web technologies.

## Features
- **Modern Aesthetics**: Dark mode "Night Club" theme with glassmorphism and neon accents.
- **Smooth Animations**: Realistic card dealing and flipping animations.
- **Interactive Betting**: Simulated casino chips ($10, $50, $100, $500).
- **Advanced Logic**:
  - Soft/Hard Hand calculation.
  - Dealer AI (Stays on 17).
  - Blackjack payout (3:2).
  - Double Down functionality.
- **Responsive Design**: Playable on desktop and mobile.

## How to Play
1. **Place your bet**: Click on the chips to add to your current bet.
2. **Deal**: Click 'DEAL' to start the round.
3. **Play Your Hand**:
   - **HIT**: Take another card.
   - **STAND**: End your turn.
   - **DOUBLE**: Double your bet and take exactly one more card.
4. **Outcome**: The dealer reveals their hidden card and must hit until they reach 17 or higher. Beat the dealer's score or wait for them to bust!

## Technical Stack
- HTML5 (Semantic Structure)
- CSS3 (Custom Properties, Flexbox, Animations)
- JavaScript (Vanilla JS, State Management)
